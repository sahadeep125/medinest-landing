How to Track Vaccination Records for Your Kids

Vaccination records always seem to be needed at the worst time — the week before school registration, a camp enrollment deadline, or a pediatrician switch. MediNest keeps a Vaccinations section on every child's profile, so the record is already there when the form asks for it.

Why a photo in your camera roll isn't enough

A photo of a vaccine card gets buried the moment you take fifty more photos that week. It's also not attached to anything — no dates parsed, no link to the child it belongs to if you have more than one kid, and no way to filter it out from vacation photos when you're searching under pressure. A dedicated Vaccinations section fixes all three problems at once.

How to log a vaccination in MediNest

  1. Open your child's profile and go to the Vaccinations tab.
  2. Add the vaccine name and date, or scan the vaccine card directly.
  3. Repeat for each dose as boosters are given.
  4. Pull up the full history any time a school, camp, or new pediatrician asks for it.

Frequently asked questions

Why do vaccination records get lost so often?

Paper vaccine cards move between pediatrician visits, school offices, and daycare enrollment forms, and rarely make it back to the same drawer twice. A digital copy filed under the child's profile removes that risk.

Can I keep vaccination records for more than one child in MediNest?

Yes. Each child gets their own profile with a dedicated Vaccinations section, so records for multiple kids never get mixed together.
