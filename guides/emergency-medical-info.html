Storing Emergency Medical Info for Your Family

In an emergency, nobody has time to reconstruct a blood type or an allergy list from memory. MediNest builds a shareable emergency card for each family member — blood group, allergies, chronic conditions, and a contact — viewable instantly by QR code, even offline.

Why emergency info needs its own system

A general medical records app is built for lookup when you have time. An emergency needs something faster — a card a babysitter, coach, school nurse, or paramedic can scan and understand in seconds, without hunting through a full profile. That's a different design problem, and it's why MediNest treats the Emergency tab as its own dedicated space.

What's on a MediNest emergency card

  • •Blood group and critical allergies. Front and center, no scrolling required.
  • •A scannable QR code. Anyone with a phone can scan and view the card instantly.
  • •One-tap call to a contact. Reach a designated emergency contact directly from the card.
  • •Share or print as PDF. Export the card for a school form, a caregiver, or a wallet copy.

Frequently asked questions

What emergency medical information should I keep on my phone?

Blood group, critical allergies, chronic conditions, current medications, and an emergency contact number — the details a first responder or school nurse would need immediately.

Does an emergency medical info card work without an internet connection?

MediNest's emergency card is designed to be viewable and shareable even offline, since emergencies don't wait for a signal.
