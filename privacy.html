Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Your family's health information is deeply personal. This Privacy Policy explains what MediNest ("we", "us") collects, how we protect it, and the choices you have. By using MediNest, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email, phone number, and password (hashed).
  • Family & health data: profiles, medical documents, prescriptions, medication schedules, and reminders you choose to add.
  • Device data: device type, OS version, crash logs, and basic usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data only to provide MediNest features: syncing across devices, sending reminders, generating doctor-visit summaries, and improving reliability. We do not sell your personal or health data.

3. Encryption & Security

All medical documents are encrypted end-to-end using AES-256. Only you and the family members you invite hold the decryption keys. MediNest staff cannot read your files.

4. Sharing & Third Parties

We share data only when you explicitly generate a share link (e.g. to a doctor), or when required by law. Links you share can be revoked at any time, and expire automatically.

5. Your Choices

  • Export or delete any family member's data at any time.
  • Delete your account permanently from Settings → Account.
  • Opt out of anonymous usage analytics in Settings → Privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

Children's records are added and managed by a parent or guardian's account. We do not knowingly allow independent accounts for users under 13.

7. Contact Us

Questions or requests about your data? Email privacy@medinest.app. We respond within 5 business days.
