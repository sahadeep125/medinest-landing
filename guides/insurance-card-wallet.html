Insurance & Emergency

Digital Insurance Card Wallet for Families

Nobody target-loads their insurance card into their phone until the day it's needed at an urgent care front desk — and it's usually the day they can't find it. A digital insurance card wallet keeps every family member's policy and ID card on your phone, organized and ready, so you're not calling home to read numbers off a card in a drawer.

Why insurance cards get lost so easily

A family of four can easily have four or more insurance cards in circulation — medical, dental, vision, supplemental — each belonging to a different person, each occasionally needed on short notice. A single photo in your camera roll works until you need last year's card, or your spouse's card while they're traveling, or your child's card for a school form. Without structure by family member, insurance cards blend into hundreds of other photos.

How MediNest's insurance wallet works

  • •Organized by family member. Every insurance card is filed under the person it belongs to, front and back.
  • •Filter by "Insurance" instantly. Pull up every policy on file across the whole family with one tap.
  • •Attached to the emergency card. Insurance details can surface alongside blood type and allergies when it matters most.

Frequently asked questions

Is it safe to keep insurance cards in a digital wallet app?

A digital insurance card wallet like MediNest keeps the card image and policy details on your device, giving you the same physical card behind your device's security instead of a photo buried in your camera roll.

Can I store insurance cards for my whole family, not just myself?

Yes. MediNest's insurance vault is organized by family member, so each person's dental, medical, or supplemental insurance card is filed separately and easy to find.
