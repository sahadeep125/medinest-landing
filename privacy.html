Privacy Policy

Effective as of 2026-01-12

This privacy policy applies to the MediNest app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Deep Saha (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a freemium service with an optional MediNest Plus subscription. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Privacy-First, On-Device Architecture

MediNest is built with your family's privacy at its core. Your family's medical records — including prescriptions, insurance cards, lab reports, vaccination history, emergency medical info, and medication schedules — are processed and stored entirely on your device. This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to external servers, and is not shared with any third parties. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health data for advertising or data brokering purposes.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects minimal information when you download and use it. Because MediNest uses an on-device architecture, the vast majority of your family's data never leaves your phone.

Data Stored On Your Device Only (Never Collected)

  • Family member profiles (name, age, sex, vitals, blood group)
  • Scanned prescriptions, lab reports, body scans, and receipts
  • Insurance cards and policy details
  • Vaccination records, allergies, and chronic condition history
  • Medication schedules, dosage, and adherence logs
  • Emergency medical information and emergency contacts

This health data is never transmitted, collected, or accessible by the Service Provider or any third party.

Limited Data Collected by Third-Party Services

MediNest may use standard third-party services to operate the app, such as anonymized product analytics (to understand which features are used, not to identify you personally) and app store billing providers (Apple App Store or Google Play Store) to process MediNest Plus subscriptions. These services may collect anonymized device information, screen view events, and purchase status — never the health documents or family records described above.

Data the Application Does NOT Collect

  • Your device's precise IP address for tracking purposes
  • Precise location data
  • The content of your scanned medical documents
  • Operating system details beyond basic device type

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device. No geolocation services are used.

Account Data (Optional)

If you create an account, the Service Provider may collect your email address for authentication and MediNest Plus subscription management purposes. For a better experience, the Service Provider may request certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to email and display name. This information is retained and used as described in this privacy policy.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application uses standard third-party services for billing and anonymized analytics, each with their own privacy practices. No family medical records or health data are shared with any of these services. Subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, subject to their respective privacy policies.

The Service Provider may disclose limited, non-health information:

  • As required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process
  • When they believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect their rights, your safety, or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request
  • With trusted service providers who work on their behalf, do not have independent use of the disclosed information, and have agreed to adhere to this privacy policy

3. Data Retention and Deletion

On-device health data — including every family member's profile, documents, and medication records — remains entirely on your device. You can delete any family member's records, or all data, at any time from within the app. When you delete data or uninstall the app, this data is permanently removed from your device.

Account data (if applicable) will be retained for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter (typically up to 30 days after account deletion for backup and legal compliance purposes). Automatically collected analytics data may be retained by third-party services according to their respective retention policies.

Upon your request for deletion, we will remove or anonymize your account data from our systems within 30 days.

4. How to Request Data Deletion

Because your family's medical records live on your device, deleting a family member's profile or your entire vault from within the app is immediate and permanent. To delete account-level data (email, subscription status), you have two options:

Option 1: In-App Account Deletion

  1. Open the MediNest application on your phone
  2. Tap on Settings in the bottom navigation bar
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen
  4. Tap on Delete Your Account to proceed

Option 2: Request by Email

If you need assistance with the deletion process or have any questions, contact us at sahadeep125@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 30 days).

5. Revoking Consent & Your Rights

You can revoke your consent and stop all collection of information by the Application by:

  • Using the in-app "Delete Your Account" option in Settings
  • Deleting individual family member profiles or documents from within the app at any time
  • Uninstalling the Application using the standard uninstall processes available on your mobile device or via the app store

6. Regulatory Compliance

The Service Provider is committed to complying with applicable data protection laws:

  • GDPR (EU/EEA): Users in the European Union have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing. You may exercise these rights by contacting us or using the deletion options above.
  • CCPA (California): California residents have the right to know what data we collect, request deletion, opt-out of sale (we do not sell personal information), and non-discrimination for exercising these rights.
  • HIPAA: MediNest is a consumer health-organization tool created and controlled by you, not a covered entity or business associate under HIPAA. Records you store are for your own personal use in managing your family's health information.
  • COPPA: The Application allows a parent or guardian to create and manage medical record profiles on behalf of their children; MediNest does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

To exercise your rights under these regulations, contact us at sahadeep125@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

MediNest is designed to let parents and guardians manage medical records for their children as part of a family vault. The Service Provider does not knowingly solicit personal information directly from children under 13, and the Application is not directed at children for independent use. Any information about a child stored in MediNest is entered and controlled by the parent or guardian's device. If you are a parent or guardian with questions about your child's data, please contact the Service Provider at sahadeep125@gmail.com.

8. Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your family's information. Your on-device health data benefits from your device's built-in security features, including device encryption and biometric or passcode protection where enabled. Because records never leave your device, they are not exposed to the risks of a centralized server breach.

Health Data Disclaimer

MediNest is a record-keeping and organization tool — not a medical device, and not a substitute for professional medical advice. The documents, reminders, and summaries you store and generate are for personal record-keeping only. Always consult a physician or qualified healthcare provider for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment decisions.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

10. Your Consent

By using the Application, you are consenting to the processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact the Service Provider via email at sahadeep125@gmail.com or visit our Support page.
