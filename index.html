FAQ

Questions, gently answered

How secure is my family's data?▾

All documents are encrypted end-to-end with AES-256. Only you and the family members you invite have access — not even MediNest can read your files.

What file types can I upload?▾

PDFs, JPGs, PNGs, HEIC photos, and common document formats (DOCX, TXT). You can also snap photos directly in the app.

How many family members can I add?▾

The free plan supports up to 4 members. MediNest Plus supports unlimited family members and shared caregiver access.

Is MediNest free?▾

Yes — the core app is free forever. MediNest Plus (unlimited members, extra storage, family sharing) starts at $4.99/month.

Can I share records with my doctor?▾

Yes — generate a temporary secure link for any doctor or specialist. Links expire automatically for privacy.
