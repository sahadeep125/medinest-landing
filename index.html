FAQ

Questions, gently answered

What is MediNest and who is it for?▾

MediNest is a family medical records app that lets you scan, store, and organize prescriptions, insurance cards, lab reports, and vaccination records for every member of your family in one vault. It's built for parents, caregivers, and anyone managing health paperwork for more than one person. Learn more →

Is my family's data secure in MediNest?▾

MediNest stores your documents and health data on your device, protected by your device's built-in security. Nothing is sold or shared without your say-so. Learn more →

Can MediNest scan and organize prescriptions automatically?▾

Yes — MediNest's medical document scanner files any prescription, insurance card, or document under the right family member and category automatically. Learn more →

How many family members can I add?▾

Add every member of your household — kids, yourself, a partner, or aging parents — and switch between their records, medications, and documents in a tap. Learn more →

Does MediNest track medication reminders?▾

Yes — MediNest's medication tracker sends dosage, schedule, and refill reminders for every prescription, for every family member. Learn more →

Can I access emergency medical info quickly?▾

MediNest generates a shareable emergency card per family member with blood group, allergies, and contacts — viewable by QR code, even offline. Learn more →

Can I store insurance cards in MediNest?▾

Yes — MediNest keeps a digital insurance card wallet organized by family member, so every policy and ID is on your phone. Learn more →

Is MediNest free?▾

Yes — MediNest is free to start with no credit card required. MediNest Plus, with unlimited family members and extra storage, comes with a 7-day free trial. Get started →
